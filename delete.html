Data & Account Deletion

Last updated: June 2026  ·  Effective immediately

The short version

  • CartChkr has no account of its own and no servers that receive your data — scanning and price comparison run entirely on your device.
  • The only personal data tied to you is your purchase record (only if you bought the paid "Move" feature), held by our payment/licence providers.
  • To delete it, email support@cartchkr.com.au with the subject "Data deletion request".
  • We action requests within 7 days and email you to confirm. No account or login is required.

1. What data exists, and who holds it

CartChkr does not operate its own servers and does not collect your cart contents, grocery lists, shopping history, or your Woolworths/Coles logins — all of that is processed on your device and is never transmitted to us. The only data associated with you is created when you make a purchase:

  • Purchase / licence record (mobile app) — when you buy "Move" (Founding, Lifetime or Monthly), the payment is processed by Google Play Billing (Android) or the Apple App Store (iOS), and your entitlement is managed by our provider RevenueCat. This record holds purchase/transaction details and an anonymous app-user identifier — not your name, address, or card number.
  • Purchase / licence record (Chrome extension) — if you purchased via the browser extension, your licence is held by ExtensionPay and payment by Stripe.
  • Support correspondence — if you contact us (e.g. via "Report an issue"), we hold that email and any diagnostic log you chose to send, only for as long as needed to help you.

2. How to request deletion

Email support@cartchkr.com.au with the subject line "Data deletion request", from (or quoting) the account/email used for the purchase. Tell us whether you used the Android app, iOS app, or Chrome extension so we can route it correctly. No app login is required.

3. What we delete, and when

  • We submit a deletion request to RevenueCat to erase your subscriber/purchase record (RevenueCat honours deletion requests).
  • We delete any support correspondence and diagnostic logs you have sent us.
  • We complete this within 7 days and email you to confirm.

Transaction records held directly by the app stores (for tax and purchase-history purposes) are controlled by those platforms. You can manage or request deletion of platform-held data via Google Play or Apple; for the extension, via ExtensionPay and Stripe. We will point you to the right place if any of these apply to you.

4. Uninstalling

Because CartChkr stores everything locally, uninstalling the app or extension removes all on-device data immediately. The only thing that remains is the purchase record described above, which you can have deleted using the process here.

5. Your rights (Australian Privacy Act)

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles, you may access, correct, or request deletion of personal information we hold about you. Email support@cartchkr.com.au.

6. Contact

Questions about deletion or your data? Email support@cartchkr.com.au. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Request deletion
