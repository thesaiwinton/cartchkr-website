FAQ

FAQ

Does it work with Woolworths Everyday Rewards prices?

Yes — CartChkr scans the prices you see when you're logged in, so member discounts and specials are factored in automatically.

Do I need to sign up for a CartChkr account?

No account, no email sign-up. CartChkr uses your existing Woolies and Coles logins — it reads the prices your browser already has access to.

Does CartChkr store my shopping data?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. We don't see your cart, your purchases, or your payment details. Ever.

What happens if an item isn't available at the other store?

CartChkr shows those items with a "no match" label so you can decide what to do. Nothing is silently dropped from your cart.

Which direction does the transfer work?

Both ways. Start at Woolies and compare to Coles, or start at Coles and compare to Woolies — whichever is cheaper wins.

What's the difference between Lifetime and Monthly?

Lifetime means you pay once and use CartChkr forever ($3.99 total, or 99¢ founding). Monthly is $2.99/month, cancel anytime — and gets early access to new store integrations as we add them.

What happens to my founding price when the extension updates?

Founding members lock in full access forever at 99¢, no matter what the price becomes. You'll always be first to get new features.

What browser does it work in?

Google Chrome — including Brave, Edge, and other Chromium-based browsers. Firefox support is on the roadmap.
